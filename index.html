英語彈性課程【寰語生活】× Context Clues 文意線索

Comprehension Skill (6): Context Clues 文意線索

When you read, you may come to words you don't know. Other words nearby may help. Look for words that mean the same or opposite. Or use details to help you figure out the meaning.

  • Context means all the words and sentences around an unknown word.
  • Context clues are hints that can help you figure out the meaning of an unknown word.

當我們在閱讀時遇到不懂的單字，可以透過前後文的線索來猜單字的大概意思，這就是 Context Clues（文意線索）。簡言之，用句子裡的提示字，幫助你猜單字意思。

▍常見的文意線索類型

類型 說明 例句
Definition
定義線索		句子會直接解釋單字意思A bilingual person, someone who can speak two languages, is often very good at communication.
Synonym
同義線索		句子裡會用相似的字來解釋The girl was joyful, or happy, when she got the gift.
Antonym
反義線索		句子裡會出現相反的字Unlike his generous brother, he is very mean.
Example
舉例線索		作者會給例子來說明單字意思She likes citrus fruits, such as oranges, lemons, and limes.
General Sense
一般語境		從上下文可以推測，沒有明確解釋He forgot to set his alarm. As a result, he was groggy and couldn't focus in class.
（groggy = 昏沉、無精神）

Lesson 16 A Musical Shape — Vocabulary Bank with examples

  1. figure ___: a shape, number, or person in a picture.
    例 The teacher drew a figure on the board.
  2. steel rod 鋼棒
    例 The fence was made of steel rods.
  3. bend into ___: to curve or shape something into a form.
    例 He bent the wire into a circle.
  4. equal ___: the same in size, amount, or value.
    例 Mom cut the cake into three equal parts.
  5. percussion ___: a type of musical instrument that you hit.
    例 The drum is a percussion instrument.
  6. space ___: an empty area or distance between things.
    例 There is enough space for everyone to sit.
  7. allow ___: to let someone do something; to make something possible.
    例 The teacher allowed us to leave early.
  8. directly ___: without anything in between; straight.
    例 Go directly to the station.
  9. block ___: to stop something from moving or prevent something from happening.
    例 The fallen tree blocked the road.
  10. instead ___: in place of something else.
    例 I didn't go out; I stayed home instead.
  11. loop ___: a circle or ring.
    例 The triangle hangs from a loop.
  12. string ___: thin rope.
    例 She tied the package with a string.
  13. float ___: to stay on the surface of a liquid or in the air.
    例 A leaf floated on the water.
  14. bar ___: a long, solid piece of metal or wood.
    例 He lifted a heavy metal bar.
  15. tap ___: to hit lightly or gently.
    例 He tapped on the door before entering.
  16. object ___: a thing that you can see or touch.
    例 There is a strange object on the table.
  17. against ___: touching or in contact with something; opposite to.
    例 He leaned against the wall.
