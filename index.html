自編講義

Lesson 16 A Musical Shape — 教學素材預覽

🔍 Comprehension Skill (6): Context Clues 文意線索
當我們在閱讀時遇到不懂的單字，可以透過前後文的線索來猜單字的大概意思，這就是 Context Clues（文意線索）。 簡言之，用句子裡的提示字，幫助你猜單字意思。
📖 Definition定義線索
句子會直接解釋單字意思
A bilingual person, someone who can speak two languages, is often very good at communication.
🔄 Synonym同義線索
句子裡會用相似的字來解釋
The girl was joyful, or happy, when she got the gift.
↔️ Antonym反義線索
句子裡會出現相反的字，幫助你理解
Unlike his generous brother, he is very mean.
📝 Example舉例線索
作者會給例子來說明單字意思
She likes citrus fruits, such as oranges, lemons, and limes.
🌐 General Sense一般語境
沒有明確解釋，但從上下文可以推測
He forgot to set his alarm. As a result, he was groggy and couldn't focus in class.
（從情境可推測 groggy = 昏沉、無精神）
📋 以 113 年會考題為例
From John Smith's book, I can see he is an advocate of bringing extinct animals back. He says we have lost many animals because we made their living spaces too dirty. He also thinks we must do everything we can to bring them back.
